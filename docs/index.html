Good Life Odyssey

Do you ever wonder what you're doing with your life? Do you question why you're working long hours? Perhaps, like me, you didn't think too much about your goals when you were younger. But as you've aged, you've started to ask deeper questions.

Being a parent and running a company have led me to contemplate how I want to live, my beliefs about how the world works, and how I know what I know. My exploration of these topics is guided principally by reading classics, incorporating new ideas from my education in physics, biomedical engineering, software, and AI.

This website is a collection of writings as I explore these topics and try to find answers for myself. Join me on my journey to find the good life.
